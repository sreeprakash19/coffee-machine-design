Introduction

NanoNeuron is an over-simplified version of the Neuron concept from Neural Networks.

NanoNeuron is trained to convert temperature values from Celsius to Fahrenheit.

For simplicity reasons we're not even going to build a network on NanoNeurons.

We will have it all working on its own, doing some magical predictions for us. Namely,

we will teach this singular NanoNeuron to convert (predict)

the temperature from Celsius to Fahrenheit.
